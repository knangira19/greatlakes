WHERE WE WORK

Why NGO Cluster

NGO Networks and Clusters have the potential to become a critical tool to coordinate and leverage existing NGO development activities in social development. They face fundamental obstacles that require creative thinking to resolve. This Cluster has the opportunity to play a central role in shaping NGO networks as effective agents In the NGO world, there is constant talk about networking and coalition building. This is especially true in the area of advocacy. To create positive change in our communities, in society and even in our workplaces, organizations and individuals need to come together to collaborate and achieve common goals. The result of this coming together and collaboration in form a network. We share information as NGO`s share information, ideas and resources to accomplish individual or group goals. Our Networking process is geared towards of acquiring resources and building power by using or creating linkages between two or more individuals, groups, or organizations. We encourage organizations to seek to build or join a network to strengthen impact of their advocacy work and ultimately impact on the ground. These networks are made up of member organizations that have come together because of shared interests.

Objectives:

Create a forum for advocacy and partnerships Enhance the rights of equal participation of each member organization in their access to resources such as donor support and technical assistance Provide a forum for the exchange and sharing of best practices and lessons learnt in women empowerment, environmental conservation, realization of social and political rights Foster partnerships and solidarity between and among members

How we work and Network

Work as a forum of updating issues and furnish member organizations with new ideas Pull together meager resources in addition to facilitating the use of resources Build capacity of members in achieving benefit Protect smaller organizations in the group Create bigger recognition and impact collectively Enhance systematic mechanism of strengthening relations and mitigate conflict Build a strong front for advocacy and learn from each other’s experiences

How we create change

The Network has divided into subgroups/task forces to take on specific tasks according to expertise. The Responsibilities are Spread across all members to reduce workload and avoid burnout.by members The Network Promotes participatory planning and decision making. We aim to foster trust and collaboration among members and Keep members motivated by acknowledging their contributions through the forum.
